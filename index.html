Is this project affiliated with Sneakerheads on the eth chain?

No we are not affiliated with them at all. This is inspired by it of course. But we are going to stick to animals for now. We are committed to bringing some of the highest quality 3D art and also give you many great reasons to want to hold your Leatherheads.

What Blockchain will this be minted on?

We will be launching on the Cardano blockchain.

Which wallet can we mint form?

You can mint from any Cardano supported wallets such as Nami, Eternl, and Daedalus. Please ensure you are not minting from an exchange wallet!

Do we own the licensing for our NFTs?

By being a holder of a Leatherhead NFT, you will have full commercial rights to that NFT.

Will there be any whitelist?

Yes! We would like to keep this as organic as possible. There will be no grinding in chat and the amount you type will not guarantee you a whitelist spot. What will help is by joining the Discord and interacting with the community. Twitter engagements will also help you get noticed.
